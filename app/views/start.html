Before you start

You can also register by post.

The online service is also available in Welsh (Cymraeg).

You can’t register for this service if you’re in the UK illegally.

You should have finished any building work on the establishment before applying for approval.

You must have the following forms in order to complete this application

  • A layout plan of the establishment
  • The food safety management system for each activity applying for (e.g. HACCP)
  • Cleaning and maintenance arrangements
  • Waste collection and disposal arrangements
  • Water supply quality testing arrangements
  • Water test result from the local water authority
  • If you are a registered business, your company registration number

Help and guidance

Please consult the Meat Industry Guide for more information on each part of the application form.

For help with completing the application form please contact the approvals team via email or telephone 01904 321321

Layout plan

These can be hand drawn but for more complicated establishments such as slaughterhouses it is advisable to contact a consultant to draw up the plans. This plan also requires a red boundary encompassing all the areas to be approved.
